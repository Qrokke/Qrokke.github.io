This is the diary corner of my research page — a place for short, informal notes
that do not need to wait for a paper: measurement quirks, reading memos,
conference impressions.

To write a new entry, I only need to add one Markdown file to `_diary/`
named `YYYY-MM-DD-some-slug.md` with a small front matter block like the one
at the top of this file. Nothing else to configure.
